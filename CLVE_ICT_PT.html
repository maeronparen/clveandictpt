What is religious freedom?

It is the power of will to determine itself and to enact itself without external and internal influence,
It also means to act without coercion or force from anybody.

What was the experience of christians during religious freedom?

Because of Religious freedom, Christians slowly started to spread wider.
in result, their community continued to grow even more
and they started to share their beliefs and practices with more people.
Soon, they gained their right to speak, live, act peacefully and share their opinions with others publicly without further consequences.
